SNAKE

how to play?

Feed the snake with as many apples as possible and watch it grow. The longer the snake's tail grows, the higher your score and the faster it slither. If the snake runs into its own tail, reverse or hit the surrounding wall, the game is over!

Use 'WASD' or 'Arrow' keys to turn the snake toward apple.

Start a New Game

Press key in any directions to awaken the snake.

Click restart to start a new game
